About HRI Metals

Welcome to our Architectual sheet metal business.

HRI Metals is a family owned and operated metal fabrication company.

Here at HRI, we specialize in crafting top-quality metal products that add durability, beauty, and function to any building. With over 35 years of experience in the industry, our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and a wide range of customized sheet metal solutions that meet your unique needs.

We take pride in our ability to work with a variety of metals

Which includs aluminum, steel, copper, and zinc. We utilize state-of-the-art techniques and equipment to fabricate metal roofing, gutters and downspouts, facade cladding, copings, flashings, and more. Our skilled craftsmen use only the highest quality materials to ensure that each product not only enhances the look of your building, but also provides reliable protection against the elements.

Our business philosophy is centered on building strong, lasting relationships with our clients.

That’s why we prioritize communication, collaboration, and transparency throughout every stage of the project. We work closely with you to understand your vision and design a solution that exceeds your expectations. From concept to completion, we stay committed to delivering the highest level of quality and craftsmanship.

At HRI, we understand that your time and budget are valuable.

That’s why we offer competitive pricing, fast turnaround times, and flexible scheduling. We also take every precaution to ensure that our work causes minimal disruption to your daily operations.

Contact our team today to learn how we can help bring your architectural sheet metal projects to life.
We look forward to hearing from you!
