HRI is a family owned and operated company with more than 30 years of experience with architectural sheet metal products. We offer custom made fabrications by converting thin metal sheets into 3-D structures.

With a wide selection of colors and finishes our sheet metal fabrication services provide a fast and cost-effective solution for any structure made from engineering drawings or custom-made fabrications. Why Metal Roofing ?

Metal roofing is also an environmentally friendly choice for several reasons. First, it is often made from recycled materials, which helps reduce waste and the demand for new resources. Additionally, metal roofs are highly reflective, helping to reduce heat absorption and lower cooling costs, which can significantly decrease energy consumption in warmer climates. Finally, metal roofs have a long lifespan—often lasting 50 years or more—minimizing the need for frequent replacements and further reducing landfill waste. Choosing metal roofing supports sustainability while providing reliable protection for your home.
